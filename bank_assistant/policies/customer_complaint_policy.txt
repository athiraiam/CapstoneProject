POLICY NAME: Customer Complaint Policy
POLICY CODE: CCP-003
LAST UPDATED: January 2024

OVERVIEW:
This policy outlines the process for receiving, recording, escalating, and resolving customer
complaints in a timely and fair manner.

DEFINITION:
A complaint is any expression of dissatisfaction from a customer regarding products, services,
staff behavior, fees, errors, or any bank-related matter.

COMPLAINT CHANNELS:
1. In-Branch: Customer visits the branch and speaks to a teller or customer service officer
2. Phone: Customer calls the bank helpline (1-800-BANK-HELP)
3. Email: complaints@bank.internal
4. Online Portal: www.bank.com/complaints
5. Written Letter: Addressed to the Branch Manager

COMPLAINT REGISTRATION:
- All complaints must be logged in the Complaint Management System (CMS) within 1 hour of receipt
- Assign a unique Complaint Reference Number (CRN) to each complaint
- Inform the customer of the CRN immediately
- Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours via customer's preferred contact method

COMPLAINT CATEGORIES:
1. Service Complaint — Poor service quality, long wait times, staff behavior
2. Transaction Complaint — Incorrect charges, failed transactions, unauthorized debits
3. Product Complaint — Issues with loans, cards, accounts, or insurance
4. Digital Complaint — Mobile app, internet banking, ATM issues
5. Staff Conduct Complaint — Misconduct, discrimination, breach of confidentiality

RESOLUTION TIMELINES:
- Simple complaints (service, minor issues): Resolve within 3 business days
- Complex complaints (transactions, fraud): Resolve within 7 business days
- Escalated complaints (legal, regulatory): Resolve within 15 business days
- Customer must be updated every 3 business days if complaint is unresolved

ESCALATION PROCESS:
Level 1: Branch Customer Service Officer
Level 2: Branch Manager (if not resolved within 3 days)
Level 3: Regional Customer Experience Manager (if not resolved within 7 days)
Level 4: Head of Customer Experience (for regulatory or legal matters)

RESOLUTION & CLOSURE:
- Inform customer of the resolution in writing (email or letter)
- Customer must confirm satisfaction or request re-escalation
- Complaint marked as "Closed" only after customer confirmation
- If customer is unsatisfied after Level 4, direct to external Banking Ombudsman

COMPENSATION POLICY:
- Verified transaction errors: Full refund + applicable interest
- Service failures: Goodwill gesture (waiver of fees, vouchers) at manager's discretion
- Fraudulent transactions (bank error): Full refund within 5 business days

RECORD KEEPING:
- All complaints and resolutions must be retained for 5 years
- Monthly complaint summary reports submitted to the Risk & Compliance team

ESCALATION CONTACT:
For unresolved complaints or escalations beyond branch level:
Contact: customerexperience@bank.internal
