POLICY NAME: Loan Policy
POLICY CODE: LP-001
LAST UPDATED: January 2024

OVERVIEW:
This policy governs the process for evaluating, approving, and disbursing loans to eligible customers.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:
- Minimum age: 21 years
- Maximum age: 60 years (at loan maturity)
- Minimum monthly income: $2,000
- Minimum credit score: 650
- Applicant must have an active bank account for at least 6 months

LOAN TYPES:
1. Personal Loan
   - Maximum amount: $50,000
   - Tenure: 1 to 5 years
   - Interest rate: 8% to 15% per annum (based on credit score)

2. Home Loan
   - Maximum amount: $500,000
   - Tenure: 5 to 30 years
   - Interest rate: 6% to 10% per annum

3. Auto Loan
   - Maximum amount: $80,000
   - Tenure: 1 to 7 years
   - Interest rate: 7% to 12% per annum

4. Business Loan
   - Maximum amount: $200,000
   - Tenure: 1 to 10 years
   - Interest rate: 9% to 16% per annum
   - Requires 2 years of business financial statements

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
- Last 3 months salary slips or income proof
- Last 6 months bank statements
- Credit report authorization form
- Loan application form (signed)

APPROVAL PROCESS:
1. Employee collects and verifies all documents
2. Credit check is run via the internal credit bureau system
3. Loan officer reviews the application (1-3 business days)
4. Approval or rejection communicated to applicant within 5 business days
5. Upon approval, loan agreement is signed
6. Disbursement within 2 business days after agreement signing

REJECTION REASONS:
- Low credit score (below 650)
- Insufficient income
- Incomplete documentation
- Existing loan default history
- High debt-to-income ratio (above 40%)

PREPAYMENT POLICY:
- Prepayment allowed after 6 months of disbursement
- Prepayment penalty: 2% of outstanding principal

ESCALATION:
For loan disputes or special cases, escalate to the Loan Operations Manager.
Contact: loan.operations@bank.internal
