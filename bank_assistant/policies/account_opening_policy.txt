POLICY NAME: Account Opening Policy
POLICY CODE: AOP-005
LAST UPDATED: January 2024

OVERVIEW:
This policy defines the requirements and steps for opening personal and business bank accounts.
All employees involved in account opening must follow this policy to ensure compliance and
customer due diligence.

ACCOUNT TYPES:
1. Savings Account
   - Minimum opening deposit: $100
   - Minimum monthly balance: $500 (below this, $10 monthly fee applies)
   - Interest rate: 1.5% to 2.5% per annum
   - Free transactions: 5 per month (charges apply beyond limit)

2. Current/Checking Account
   - Minimum opening deposit: $200
   - No minimum balance required (business customers)
   - Monthly fee: $15 (waived for balances above $2,000)
   - Unlimited transactions included

3. Fixed Deposit Account
   - Minimum deposit: $1,000
   - Tenure: 30 days to 5 years
   - Interest rate: 3% to 6% per annum (based on tenure)
   - Premature withdrawal penalty: 1% reduction in interest rate

4. Joint Account
   - Available for savings and current accounts
   - Both applicants must complete full KYC
   - Mode of operation: Either or Survivor / Both to Sign

5. Business Account
   - Minimum opening deposit: $500
   - Requires corporate KYC documentation
   - Dedicated relationship manager assigned

ELIGIBILITY:
Personal Accounts:
- Minimum age: 18 years
- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of address
- Initial deposit as per account type

Minor Accounts (under 18):
- Parent or guardian must be joint account holder
- Guardian must complete full KYC
- Converted to regular account at age 18

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS (Personal):
- Government-issued photo ID (Passport, Driver's License, National ID)
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement - within 3 months)
- Completed account opening form
- Initial deposit (cash or cheque)
- Passport-size photograph (2 copies)

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS (Business):
- Certificate of incorporation or business registration
- Memorandum and Articles of Association
- Board resolution authorizing account opening
- List of authorized signatories with specimen signatures
- KYC for all directors (ID + address proof)
- Proof of business address
- Tax Identification Number (TIN)

ACCOUNT OPENING PROCESS:
1. Customer approaches branch or applies via mobile/web portal
2. Employee collects and verifies all required documents
3. KYC verification completed in KYCMS
4. Account opening form submitted to the system
5. Account created and account number generated within 1 business day
6. Welcome kit (debit card, cheque book, welcome letter) dispatched within 5 business days
7. Customer receives SMS and email confirmation

DEBIT CARD:
- Issued automatically with savings and current accounts
- Card delivered within 5 business days
- Default daily limit: $1,000 (can be increased up to $5,000 via app or branch)
- PIN set by customer via ATM or mobile app

ONLINE BANKING ACCESS:
- Internet banking credentials provided via email upon account activation
- Mobile app linked after first login verification
- 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) mandatory

DORMANT ACCOUNTS:
- Account becomes dormant after 12 months of no transactions
- Customer notified by email/SMS before dormancy
- To reactivate: customer must visit branch with valid ID and address proof

ACCOUNT CLOSURE:
- Customer must visit branch with valid ID
- All outstanding balances, loans, or card dues must be cleared
- Closure form to be signed
- Funds transferred to customer's nominated account within 2 business days

ESCALATION:
For account opening exceptions or complex cases:
Contact: accountoperations@bank.internal
