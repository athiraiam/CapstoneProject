POLICY NAME: Credit Card Policy
POLICY CODE: CCP-004
LAST UPDATED: January 2024

OVERVIEW:
This policy covers the issuance, usage, billing, disputes, and cancellation of credit cards
offered by the bank to eligible customers.

ELIGIBILITY:
- Minimum age: 21 years
- Minimum monthly income: $3,000 (for standard cards)
- Minimum monthly income: $6,000 (for premium/platinum cards)
- Minimum credit score: 680
- Must have an active savings or current account with the bank

CREDIT CARD TYPES:
1. Classic Card
   - Credit limit: Up to $5,000
   - Annual fee: $50 (waived if annual spend exceeds $6,000)
   - Rewards: 1 point per $1 spent

2. Gold Card
   - Credit limit: Up to $15,000
   - Annual fee: $150 (waived if annual spend exceeds $15,000)
   - Rewards: 2 points per $1 spent
   - Complimentary airport lounge access: 2 visits/year

3. Platinum Card
   - Credit limit: Up to $50,000
   - Annual fee: $300 (waived if annual spend exceeds $30,000)
   - Rewards: 3 points per $1 spent
   - Unlimited airport lounge access
   - Complimentary travel insurance

APPLICATION PROCESS:
1. Customer fills out credit card application form
2. Employee verifies identity and income documents
3. Credit bureau check is performed
4. Approval by credit card department within 3 business days
5. Card dispatched via secure courier within 5 business days of approval

BILLING:
- Billing cycle: Monthly (1st to last day of month)
- Statement generated on the 1st of each month
- Payment due date: 20th of the same month
- Minimum payment: 5% of outstanding balance or $25 (whichever is higher)

INTEREST RATES:
- Standard APR: 18% to 24% per annum (based on credit profile)
- Cash advance APR: 28% per annum
- Late payment fee: $35 per occurrence
- Over-limit fee: $25 per occurrence

CARD USAGE LIMITS:
- Cash advance limit: 30% of credit limit
- International transaction limit: Subject to customer's travel notification

LOST/STOLEN CARD:
- Customer must report immediately to: 1-800-CARD-STOP
- Card will be blocked within 15 minutes of report
- Liability for unauthorized transactions: Customer not liable if reported promptly
- Replacement card issued within 5 business days

DISPUTE PROCESS:
1. Customer raises dispute via app, phone, or branch
2. Dispute logged in system with reference number
3. Bank investigates within 7 business days
4. If fraud confirmed: Full refund and new card issued
5. If merchant error: Chargeback initiated

CARD CANCELLATION:
- Customer can cancel anytime by calling helpline or visiting branch
- Outstanding balance must be cleared before cancellation
- Reward points must be redeemed before cancellation (non-transferable)
- Annual fee is non-refundable after card activation

ESCALATION:
For credit card disputes or limit enhancement requests:
Contact: creditcards@bank.internal
